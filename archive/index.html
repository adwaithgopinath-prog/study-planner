Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI study plan work? +
You input your syllabus, exam date, and available study hours. Claude AI analyzes the content, estimates time per topic, and builds a day-by-day schedule with session lengths and built-in revision cycles.
Is the free plan really free? +
Yes — forever. You get 3 AI study plans, the full Pomodoro timer, session tracker, and basic analytics with no credit card required.
What subjects does StudyForge support? +
Any subject. Physics, Maths, Chemistry, Law, Medicine, History, CS — paste your syllabus in any language and the engine handles it.
Where is my AI data processed? +
All AI processing happens locally on your machine via Ollama. Your study material never leaves your device.
